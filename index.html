What's in a name?

A lot! A name has so many characteristics.

Amongst the most obvious ones are the length, the origin and the meaning. Vik is close to Vic, but very different compared to Antonio don't you think?

Another interesting characteristic is the popularity of the name over time. You can use this time series to give names labels such as popular or trending.

We also use all the historical clicks on the website. Users clicking on Guillaume also often click on Antoine, hence we know that these two are related to each other.

We combine the characteristics of a name and the historical clicks, to build a recommender system. This is a very well known subject in the data science field.

We hope you liked it!
